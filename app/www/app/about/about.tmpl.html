WirelessHART Hero is a responsive mobile web application developed with the Ionic Framework and HART-IP, allowing you to easily connect to a WirelessHART Gateway and quickly interact with and visualize the collective dataset of a WirelessHART wireless mesh. This product was developed by Kevin Mershon and Cristopher Pascua of Mershon Enterprises LLC, and Darin Hoover of CodeNet.io.

WirelessHART is an IEEE standard developed by the HART Communication Foundation, first introduced in 2007. All copyrights belong to their respective owners.
