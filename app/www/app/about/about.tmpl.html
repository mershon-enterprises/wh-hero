WH Hero is a mobile application developed with the Ionic Framework and HART-IP, allowing you to easily connect to a WirelessHART Gateway and quickly interact with and visualize the collective dataset of a WirelessHART wireless mesh. This product was developed by Kevin Mershon and Cristopher Pascua of Mershon Enterprises LLC, and Darin Hoover of CodeNet.io.
